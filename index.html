My Story

Not a resume. A journey.

Origin

Beginning in Nazareth

A small village in southern India shaped the foundation of who I am — curiosity, resilience, and the belief that opportunity is something you build, not something you're given.

Early Career

Learning the ground floor

I started where most people don't see the cameras — understanding talent, people, and business from the inside out, one hire and one decision at a time.

Growth

Learning leadership

Leadership isn't a title — it's a discipline. I learned it by building teams, making hard calls, and earning trust long before I earned authority.

Scale

Building teams that build companies

Over 17 years, I've built recruitment functions, scaled organizations, and designed people strategies that turned hiring from a bottleneck into a competitive advantage.

Inflection

Discovering AI

I saw AI not as a threat to people functions, but as the most powerful lever ever handed to leaders who care about both performance and people.

What's Next

Future vision

Helping organizations build the future of work — where AI amplifies human potential, and where people remain the center of every great business.
